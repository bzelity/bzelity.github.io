Balazs Zelity

I am a PhD candidate in economics at Brown University. I primarily do policy-relevant research on medium- and long-run economic growth and macroeconomics. My other fields of interest include international and population economics.

Methodologically, in addition to employing empirical techniques from applied microeconomics, I also extensively work with general equilibrium models using a mixture of mathematical analysis and computational simulation.
